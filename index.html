Wound Threshold: Brawn + Species Bonus
Strain Threshold: Willpower + Species Bonus
Defense: Armor + Talents
Soak: Brawn + Armor
Species

  • Human: +2 to any two characteristics, 110 XP
  • Bothan: +1 Cunning, +1 Intellect, 100 XP
  • Duros: +1 Intellect, +1 Presence, 100 XP
  • Mon Calamari: +1 Intellect, +1 Education, 100 XP
  • Sullustan: +1 Agility, +1 Intellect, 100 XP
  • Twi'lek: +1 Cunning, +1 Presence, 100 XP
Combat

Initiative Order:
1. Roll Initiative (Cool or Vigilance)
2. Arrange by Success, then Advantage

Actions per Turn:
• 1 Action + 1 Maneuver
• 2 Maneuvers (suffer 2 strain)
• Multiple Incidentals

Damage: Base Damage + uncanceled Success
Critical: Advantage = Weapon's Crit Rating
Soak: Brawn + Armor Soak Value
Defense: Upgrade difficulty dice
Vehicles

Silhouette: Size category (0-10)
Speed: Maximum speed
Handling: Maneuverability modifier
Hull Trauma: Damage capacity
System Strain: System damage capacity

Pilot Only Actions: Pilot, Copilot
Gunner Actions: Fire ship weapons
Engineer Actions: Damage control, boost systems
Passenger Actions: Limited options
